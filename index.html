Chicken Biryani

The traditional method of cooking chicken biryani involves a technique known as “dum.” This involves sealing the pot tightly and cooking the biryani over low heat, allowing the flavors to meld and develop. The result is a succulent and flavorful dish that showcases the culinary artistry of India.
